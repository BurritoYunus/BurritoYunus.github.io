What is
BIG DATA?

"You can't manage what you can't measure." - you can not control without data.

With BIG DATA we are talking about large amounts of quantitative data, and data itself is basically a management and decision making tool.

How dangerous is
BIG DATA?

Simply said BIG DATA is the digital footprints that we leave behind, both online and offline

Every time we use our credit cards.
Every time we search on the internet.
Every move we take with our phones
in our pockets.
Every time we click "like".
Everything is saved.
